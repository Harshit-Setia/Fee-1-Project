SAMBHAR

Material Required:

  1. 3/4 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  2. 2 cups water to cook dal
  3. 1 to 2 tablespoon tamarind or tamarind paste as needed.
  4. 1 tablespoon jaggery (optional)
  5. 3/4 tablespoon salt (adjust to taste)
  6. 1/4 teaspoon turmeric (skip if your sambar powder has it)
  7. 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon red chilli powder (optional)
  8. 1/4 cup coriander leaves chopped

RASAM

Material Required:

  1. 2 to 3 medium tomatoes or (1 to 11/2 cups) chopped or pureed
  2. 1/2teaspoon turmeric
  3. 1/2 to 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. 21/2 to 3 cups water
  5. 1 teaspoon Tamarind (optional, use as needed) or lemon juice
  6. 1 to 2 tablespoon jaggery or sugar (or as needed)
  7. 1/2 cup coriander leaves with stems chopped finely
