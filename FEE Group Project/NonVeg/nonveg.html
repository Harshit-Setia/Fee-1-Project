MUTTON GHEE ROAST

Material Required:

  3. 1/2teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi)

  5. 6 Dry Red Chillies,salt
  6. 2 Cloves (Laung)
  7. 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns
  8. 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds)
  9. 2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds
  10. 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera)
  11. 6 cloves Garlic
